About Us

We are a small start up driven by a need to give back to programs which aid underserved youths. Both Co-founders are first generation college graduates, children of immigrants, who were guided through their educational journey; which is what fuels our passion as a company to aid organizations who are helping young people find their way through school. Co-Founder Arturo Montoya is a proud graduate of San Jose State University with a B.S. in Computer Science and Co-Founder Diego Maciel is a proud graduate of Saint Mary’s College of California with a B.A. in History.
