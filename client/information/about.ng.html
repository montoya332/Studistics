We are a small start up driven by a need to give back to programs which aid underserved youths. Both Co-founders are first generation college graduates, children of immigrants, who were guided through their educational journey; which is what fuels our passion as a company to aid organizations who are helping young people find their way through school. Co-Founder Arturo Montoya is a proud to be the first in his family to graduate from college with a B.S. in Computer Engineering from San Jose State University and Co-Founder Diego Maciel is a also the first in his family to graduate from college with a B.A. in History from Saint Mary’s College of California.

Arturo Montoya

Co-Founder Arturo Montoya is a proud to be the first in his family to graduate from college with a B.S. in Computer Engineering from San Jose State University. Arturo originally comes from a background of working with underserved youth in the south bay and the peninsula. From his time at San Jose State University emerged with a passion for driving student success through efficiency and innovation. Over the last year he has worked on various projects aiming to improve tracking efficiency for both the non-profit and government sectors.

Diego Maciel

Co-Founder Diego Maciel is also the first in his family to graduate from college with a B.A. in History from Saint Mary’s College of California. Over the past three years he has been working extensively with underserved youths throughout the Bay Area with various non-profit organizations. After these years of on the field work he realized the need for a database tracking system designed around the everyday needs of organizations working with youths, in order to evaluate data that is being collected in an effective and efficient way.
